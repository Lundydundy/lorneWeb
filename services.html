Regulatory Audit Support

When your firm faces a securities regulatory audit, it becomes a crucial and time-intensive undertaking for both management and staff. Lorne efficiently collaborates with regulators, guiding your firm through the audit process and addresses any identified deficiencies with expertise.

New Dealer/Adviser Registration

Services provided:

  • Compiling and submitting a comprehensive registration package to the designated Canadian regulator, adhering to National Instrument 31-103 Registration Requirements, Exemptions, and Ongoing Registrant Obligations, long with SRO Rules.

  • Facilitating the completion and submission of necessary forms for firm setup through the National Registration Database (NRD).

  • Crafting the Compliance Policies & Procedures Manual, Business Continuity Plan, and "Know-Your-Client" documentation.

  • Developing the Anti-Money Laundering Program and Risk Assessment for the firm.

  • Finally, handling the completion and submission of registration applications for key personnel, including the qualified UDP, CCO, permitted individuals, supervisory personnel, and representatives of the new firm applicant.
